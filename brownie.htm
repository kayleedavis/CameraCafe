  • Kodak Brownie Model D

  • 620 SLR film
  • Black and white film
  • 100mm lens
  • The Brownie camera was produced in the UK in 1937 and was discontinued in 1957. There is a specific wheel that must be used in order to put the film in the camera, which can either house 35mm or 620 film. Unlike most cameras, the viewfinder is on the top of the camera, which causes you to look down as you take a photo.
